Want to get involved?

Our events page is a hub of activities dedicated to fostering awareness, education, and engagement in sustainable farming, agriculture, and environmental stewardship. Check out some events happening around you to help out with making our environment better!

Popular Events

Here are some popular events that have been carried out over the years...

  • 1945

    World Food Day

    World Food Day has been around since 1945 and is celebrated annually on October 16th till this day. It aims to raise awareness about global food security and hunger issues. Events include conferences, workshops, and initiatives promoting sustainable agriculture practices and equitable access to food.

  • 1970

    Organic World Congress

    The Organic World Congress has been held every three years since the 1970s. The specific year of inception may vary, but the event has a long history of bringing together the global organic farming community.

  • 2015

    International Year of Soils

    The United Nations declared 2015 as the International Year of Soils to raise awareness about the importance of soil for food security, ecosystem services, and sustainable agriculture. The year-long campaign aimed to promote the sustainable management of soil resources.

  • 2020-2023

    The Sustainable Agriculture Summit

    The Sustainable Agriculture Summit has been an ongoing annual event, with the first summit taking place in recent years. It focuses on contemporary issues and advancements in sustainable agriculture practices.
